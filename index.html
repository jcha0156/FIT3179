Vehicle Registrations in Malaysia (2024) - Data Visualizations

This data visualization project provides insights into vehicle registrations in Malaysia using three key visualizations. The project highlights the distribution of vehicle registrations across different states, the trend of monthly vehicle registrations by type, and the usage of various fuel types in registered vehicles. This map shows the total number of vehicles registered across each state in Malaysia. States are color-coded to represent the number of registered vehicles, where darker colors indicate a higher number of registrations. Hover over any state to view the exact registration count.

This line chart tracks the number of vehicles registered each month in 2024, categorized by vehicle type. It reveals that motorcars are the most popular type of vehicle, showing a consistently higher registration rate. You can filter the chart by vehicle type to focus on a specific category.

This bar chart illustrates the distribution of vehicles based on the type of fuel they use. Petrol is the most widely used fuel, making up the majority of vehicles, while alternative fuel types such as hybrid, electric and diesel vehicles are used in smaller quantities. The chart offers insights into fuel preferences among registered vehicles in Malaysia.
